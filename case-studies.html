Examples

Representative TaskyHub workflow patterns.

These are product-aligned examples of the kinds of workflows the TaskyHub SaaS app is intended to support.

Google workspace onboarding

A customer admin connects Google for one workspace, TaskyHub validates credentials, stores the refresh token for that workspace, and unlocks Google-backed actions in the builder.

Support intake and AI routing

Inbound requests are captured, classified with AI, tagged by urgency, and routed to the correct queue with saved run history.

Saved data driven workflows

Teams maintain reusable links, snippets, and endpoint definitions once, then reference them across multiple automations.

Operational outcome

  • Fewer broken automations caused by mixed workspace state
  • Clearer ownership of credentials and provider activation
  • Faster support because failures are visible per workspace

Product principle

  • Keep tenant routing explicit
  • Keep canonical infrastructure stable
  • Keep error messages actionable for admins and builders

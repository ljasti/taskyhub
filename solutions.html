Use Cases

TaskyHub supports practical, tenant-safe automation.

The product is designed for repeatable workflows that combine integrations, AI steps, operational data, and clear workspace boundaries.

Customer onboarding

Set up credentials, collect requirements, trigger approvals, and launch workspace-specific flows.

Support and triage

Capture incoming issues, classify them with AI, route to the right team, and keep audit trails.

Lead and revenue ops

Sync form submissions, enrich records, update CRM systems, and trigger personalized outbound actions.

For platform teams

  • Manage customer workspaces from a central SaaS platform.
  • Enforce operational rules and secure shared infrastructure.
  • Keep integrations and secrets cleanly segmented.

For customer admins

  • Activate the providers their workspace actually uses.
  • Review saved data, endpoints, and workflow-related settings.
  • Test integrations without affecting other tenants.

For builders

  • Reuse endpoint and saved-data assets across workflows.
  • Compose AI actions with operational context.
  • Inspect runs and refine automations faster.

For operations and success teams

  • View workspace health and run outcomes.
  • Spot failed integrations before customers do.
  • Support multi-tenant customers without shared-state mistakes.
Why this matters

Automation is only useful when it stays reliable per customer workspace.

TaskyHub focuses on correct workspace propagation, clear provider ownership, and safe auth patterns so the same platform can serve many customers without mixing their data.

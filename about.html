About

TaskyHub is being built for teams that need automation without tenant risk.

The goal is simple: make it easier to ship AI-enabled workflows while keeping workspace boundaries, integration credentials, and operational controls explicit and safe.

What TaskyHub stands for

  • Automations should be understandable, not black boxes.
  • Workspace-scoped data should stay workspace-scoped.
  • Integrations should be easy to connect and safe to operate.
  • AI features should be practical and observable in production.

Who it is for

  • SaaS teams managing many customer workspaces
  • Operations teams replacing fragile manual steps
  • Builders creating AI-assisted workflows and endpoints
  • Admins who need secure integration governance
